    Model Viewing/Creating

    Requirements

    • GT2ModelTool by pez2k
    • GT2TextureEditor by pez2k
    • For simply viewing models, GT2CarViewer by Leo2236

    Workflow

    If you only wish to view models, use GT2CarViewer.

    For creation/details, refer to SUBMANIAC's document, and his youtube tutorial stream.
